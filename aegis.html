Aegis

Lightweight security operations accelerator—playbooks, automations, and reporting that plug into your stack.

See Features Request a Demo

Outcomes

  • Faster findings-to-fixes cycles
  • Repeatable evidence & metrics
  • Team enablement via guided runbooks

Engagement Playbooks

Prebuilt workflows for pen tests, purple-team sprints, and IR readiness with artifact templates and acceptance checks.

Automation Hooks

Simple webhooks/CLI glue to push issues to Jira/GitHub, update dashboards, and notify Slack/MS Teams.

Reporting Accelerator

Executive and technical report scaffolds with evidence blocks, MITRE mappings, and remediation matrices.

How Aegis Fits

Aegis is stack-agnostic. Use it alongside your existing vulnerability scanners, EDR, SIEM, and ticketing.

  • Inputs: findings JSON/CSV, notes, screenshots, logs
  • Outputs: issues, dashboards, reports, metrics
  • Integrations: Jira, GitHub, GitLab, Slack/Teams, CSV/JSON
