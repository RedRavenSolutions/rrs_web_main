About Red Raven Solutions

We’re a small, focused cybersecurity firm helping teams strengthen defenses through practical testing, collaboration, and measurable outcomes.

Our Mission

Red Raven Solutions was founded on a simple idea: security work should deliver clarity and impact, not noise and confusion. We help organizations uncover real attack paths, validate detection coverage, and make changes that actually reduce risk.

Whether through penetration testing, red teaming, or ongoing advisory, our goal is to shorten the distance between discovery and improvement.

Our Approach

  • Focus on realistic, goal-oriented testing—not checkbox exercises.
  • Communicate findings in plain language executives can act on.
  • Work collaboratively with defenders to improve detection and response.
  • Emphasize repeatability, transparency, and partnership.

Leadership

Red Raven Solutions is led by professionals with decades of experience in cybersecurity, IT, and threat operations. We’ve worked across government, defense, and commercial sectors—bridging the gap between technical depth and strategic execution.

Core Values

  • Integrity: Say what’s true, not what’s convenient.
  • Clarity: Turn findings into decisions, not confusion.
  • Partnership: Treat every engagement like a collaboration.
  • Excellence: Deliver work we’d be proud to defend in a review.

Where We’re Based

Headquartered in Prince William County, Virginia. Supporting clients across the U.S. and select international partners remotely.
